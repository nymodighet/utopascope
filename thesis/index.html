Thesis

This project explores the ways that stronger communities can build better systems for the future. It seeks new ways to manage, to spend leisure time, to work, and to imagine new markets so that the old patterns and circumstances that keep us all down can be replaced. Each part of this project aims to make life better for all, even those who disagree with us or who we might think of as those who are on “the other side.”

The vision of the world presented in this project is one in which people self-organize around shared interests and needs, all people of the world have clean air and water and have access to mental and physical health services, direct democracy is embedded in daily life, and economies are liberated so that all people may have the freedom to be happy.
