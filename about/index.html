About Us

The Design Activist Institute is an intersectional, autonomous, grassroots collective of designers working for radical, utopian resistance and building a better world. The core of our approach is a commitment to co-creating with communities and generating creative cooperation with our fellow members of the working class. In total, 21 of us contributed to this project at varying points, infusing the design collective with democratic decision-making and consensus-building to visualize and organize for a better future.

Have any suggestions for new community organizing practices? Would you like to submit new long view content to the project? Do you have suggestions to revise the practices in ways that might improve them for all? Reach out to the Design Activist Institute with any questions or concerns.
