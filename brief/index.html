Brief

Utopascope consists of conversations that show what our ideal worlds look like and what steps people like you can take to start getting there today. These processes explore community organizing as a way to return power to the people. Some of the goals of this project are to share process information about our organization and design, and to create an organizing project out of these practices based on community response.

This project is meant to continuously grow, involving the community through an ongoing exchange of information and ideas. With your help, this will expand into tangible, organized direct action so that we build a better future. We are continuously adding new interviews, essays, audio, video, and explorations based on community creation. We invite you to propose new community organizing practices, suggest changes to existing ones, and to partner with us to create a revolutionary moment.

August 31, 2021 press release
